I Hate You

Evan Michael Brown

Composer

As a member of the band Shaimus, Evan's music and guitar playing appeared in video games (GUITAR HERO, ROCK BAND 2), TV shows (ONE TREE HILL), and the Sony/Screen Gems film, THE ROOMMATE. He has since branched out into composing music for short films, web video, podcasts, and commercials. I HATE YOU is his first feature-length score. In addition to music, he enjoys writing for his blog and drawing cartoon dinosaurs in whis webcomic.
