I Hate You

Synopsis

Kelly is tired, stuck in a cubicle, and lives with her ex.

Chi is inexperienced, unemployed, and still lives with his mom.

When they meet, it’s... well, it’s not quite love at first sight. But they give it a shot anyway. On the battlefield of love, which will prevail - Kelly’s pragmatism or Chi’s idealism? And can their relationship survive either one?
